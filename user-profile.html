6 DAY WORKOUT SPLIT


Monday: Push Focus - Chest and Triceps

Tuesday: Pull Focus - Back and Biceps

Wednesday: Leg Day - Hamstrings Focus

Thursday: Shoulders and Abs

Friday: Push/Pull Combos

Saturday: Leg Day - Quad Focus

Sunday: Rest day

DAY 1: PUSH - CHEST AND TRICEPS

Looking to build a strong and defined upper body? A push-focused workout targeting your chest and triceps can help you achieve just that! By incorporating a combination of compound movements and isolation exercises, you can effectively train these muscle groups and improve their strength and size. Let's dive in!

Workout Reps Sets Rest time
Barbell Bench Press 8-12 3 2 minutes
Dumbbell Flyes 10-15 3-4 90 seconds
Incline Dumbbell Press 8-12 4-5 2 minutes
TRX Suspended Push-Ups 10-15 3 60 seconds
RIP Trainer Chest Press 10-15 3 60 seconds
Tricep Dips 8-12 4-5 2 minutes
TRX Tricep Extension 8-12 3 60 seconds

DAY 2: PULL-BACK AND BICEPS

Build a strong back and biceps with this pull-focused workout! Not only will you see improvements in strength and muscle definition, but you'll also improve your posture and decrease your risk of injury. Grab some weights, a suspension trainer and a TRX rip trainer, and let's get after it!

Workout Reps Sets Rest time
TRX Low Rows 10-15 3 45 seconds
Bent-Over Barbell Rows 8-12 4 60-90 seconds
Pull-Ups 6-10 3 2 minutes
Single-Arm Dumbbell Rows 10-12/arm 3 45 seconds
TRX Rip Trainer High Row 12-15 3 45 seconds
Barbell Curls 8-12 3 60 seconds
Hammer Curl 8-12 3 60 seconds

DAY 3: LEG DAY - HAMSTRINGS

It's time to switch the focus from your upper body to the lower body with this hamstring-focused workout! Strong and flexible hamstrings are essential for overall lower body strength and athletic performance. So let's dive in and get those hamstrings firing

Workout Reps Sets Rest time
Romanian Deadlifts 8-12 4 60-90 seconds
Single-Leg Glute Bridges 10-12/leg 3 45-60 seconds
TRX Hamstring Curls 12-15 3 45-60 seconds
Nordic Hamstring Curls 6-8 3 60-90 seconds
Seated Leg Curl Machine 8-12/leg 3 45-60 seconds
Barbell Hip Thrusts 8-12 3 2 minutes

DAY 4: SHOULDERS AND ABS

Improve your posture, stability, and overall upper body strength with this shoulder and core-focused session. Whether you're an athlete looking to enhance performance or a fitness enthusiast seeking a challenging workout, this routine will help you achieve your goals. Get ready to engage your core and sculpt your shoulders with the following exercises

Workout Reps Sets Rest time
Shoulder Press 10-12 3 60 seconds
Plank Hold for 30-60 seconds 3 30 seconds
TRX Pike 10-12 3 30 seconds
Arnold Press 8-10 3 60 seconds
TRX Side Plank 20-30s hold/side 3 45 seconds
RIP Trainer Plank 30-40s hold 3 45 seconds

DAY 5: PUSH PULL SUPERSETS

Push-pull supersets are an excellent way to target multiple muscle groups efficiently while keeping your workout dynamic and challenging. By combining pushing and pulling movements, you engage your chest, shoulders, back, and arms in a balanced and effective manner. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced fitness enthusiast, these exercises can help you achieve your upper body goals.

Workout Reps Sets Rest time
TRX Chest Press & TRX Mid Row 12-15 3 45 seconds between sets
Push-ups & Seated Cable Rows 10-12 3 60s seconds between sets
Close-Grip Bench Press & Lat Pulldowns 10-12 3 60s seconds between sets
Dumbbell Overhead Tricep Extensions & Barbell Bicep Curls 12-15 3 45 seconds between sets

DAY 6: LEG DAY - QUAD FOCUS

Looking to sculpt strong and defined quads? Look no further than this leg-day quad-focused workout. By targeting your quadriceps, you can build lower body strength, improve stability, and enhance athletic performance. For this leg day, you'll mostly use barbells, dumbbells, and some leg workout machines. However, building a lower body requires flexibility and muscle coordination. This is why we'll include some bodyweight exercises using the TRX suspension trainer.

Workout Reps Sets Rest time
Barbell Back Squats 8-12 4 2 minutes
Dumbbell Lunges 10-12 3/td> 2 minutes
Leg Press 12-15 3 1-2 minutes
Bulgarian Split Squats 10-12/leg 3 60 seconds
TRX Pistol Squats 8-10/leg 3 1-2 minutes
Calf Raises (Machine or Standing) 15-20 3 1-2 minutes
TRX Rip Trainer Knee Strike 12-15/leg 3 60 seconds
