Member Benefits

WE MAKE IT EASY TO STAY MOTIVATED AND KEEP FIT

With fitness experts always on hand, an advanced range of workout options, the latest exercise equipment and classes that fit seamlessly around your busy lifestyle, you can train on your terms at The Atlethic Fitness.

Join on a Passport membership and get access to more than 91 The Atlethic Fitness clubs throughout Indonesia, Malaysia, Philippines, Singapore and Thailand. We’re open long hours, 7 days a week so you never have an excuse not to train.

We have the latest machines for cardio exercise and weight training as well as Freestyle training innovations such as Kettlebells and Suspension Exercise, offering variety to keep you motivated.

Fun, motivating and social, our wide variety of Group Exercise classes make it easy to reach your fitness goals. HIIT X, Pro-Cycling, Zumba, BodyPump, Yoga and more - they're available for members to add on to their gym membership for a minimal fee. Plus, we release new classes regularly to keep things fresh and interesting.

Join our HIIT Circuit workouts where a qualified trainer will take groups of members through a fun, interactive 30 minutes or more structured workout on the gym floor.

Enjoy complimentary beverage at selected clubs while you relax at our member's lounge. Our clubs also offer free WiFi access and internet stations.

LET’S START THE JOURNEY

Here at The Atlethic Fitness we are committed to providing a great workout experience for our members, so that everyone can get fit while enjoying our endless flow of great privileges.

LEVEL 1 : NEW JOINED MEMBER

Enjoy exclusive discounts from our partner merchants.

Ready to get started? This is your best option. Avail of three (3) or six (6) personalized 60-minute sessions with a Certified Fitness Coach at a special rate.

Newly joined members are ensured that they are given a proper start with their fitness journey through a New Member Induction. Enjoy one (1) complimentary 60-minute BodyStart sessions upon signing up.

LEVEL 2 : 3 TO 10 MONTHS

Receive two (2) one-week guest passes when you reach your 3rd month.

Enjoy a complimentary Boost session for non-PT clients, or a 10% PT discount for existing PT clients on your 9th month.

Refer a friend and receive a P500 voucher for every successful referral on your 6th month.

LEVEL 3 : 10 MONTHS ++

50% off your membership dues for every successful referral made on your 12th month.

Avail of exclusive promotions on The Atlethic Fitness services and products.

Receive special invites to The Atlethic Fitness events.

ALL TIME BENEFITS

Receive a gift throughout the year by referring your friends. Every successful referral entitles you to a special reward.

Traveling? Don’t let your travel plans intervene with your fitness routine. You are entitled up to thirty (30) International Passport visits per calendar year in accordance with your membership type. Speak to our Front of House team for details.
